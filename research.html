Welcome to Our Research Page


Research

Our research endeavors are structured around three primary themes: Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, and Systems. Each theme encompasses distinct research objectives and presents its own set of challenges. The faculty members within our organization concentrate on generating influential contributions to the field as a whole. They achieve this by targeting significant problems within these major directions, thus addressing numerous vital societal applications. Although our research areas are classified under these themes, many of them cover a diverse array of sub-topics and practical applications. As a result, they often transcend the boundaries between these categories, reflecting the interdisciplinary nature of our work.


Artificial Intelligence ( A.I)

The field of artificial intelligence (AI) research centers around the exploration and development of methods that enable machines to understand and carry out tasks that traditionally require human intelligence. AI encompasses a broad spectrum of techniques and methodologies, including machine learning, deep learning, natural language processing, knowledge representation, computer vision, robotics, and expert systems. These diverse approaches collectively contribute to advancing our understanding and capabilities in the realm of AI.Our research encompasses a broad spectrum of topics within the dynamic and rapidly evolving field of machine learning. We strive to advance the understanding and capabilities of machines in various aspects, including learning, prediction, control, security, robustness, and trustworthiness. Our research efforts span both the theoretical foundations and practical applications of machine learning.In this expansive domain, our investigations delve into areas such as ML theory, which includes the study of algorithms and optimization techniques. We also explore statistical learning, focusing on inference, graphical models, and causal analysis.


Data Science (DS)

Research in Data Science centers around the utilization of statistical and computational techniques to extract valuable insights and knowledge from data. Within our department, this field encompasses various areas of research, including databases, human-computer interaction, visualization, and theoretical computer science.Our researchers explore innovative approaches and methodologies to handle and analyze large datasets, aiming to uncover meaningful patterns, trends, and relationships. They delve into database management systems, developing efficient methods for data storage, retrieval, and processing. Additionally, they investigate human-computer interaction to enhance the usability and effectiveness of data analysis tools. Visualization techniques are also explored to present data in a visually intuitive and informative manner.Furthermore, our researchers delve into theoretical computer science to develop robust algorithms and mathematical models that underpin data science methodologies. By combining expertise from these diverse areas, our department strives to advance the field of Data Science and unlock its potential for generating valuable insights from complex datasets.
